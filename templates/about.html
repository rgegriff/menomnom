Our Mission
Our mission is to make it easy for Menomonie community members to find information that pertains to their day-to-day lives. Furthermore, we believe that local businesses are at the heart of a strong community, and so we do everything we can to concentrate our efforts locally.
Our Story
During the summer of 2011, we had an idea. We wanted to create a website that not only acted as a central hub of information for members of the Menomonie community (something that was lacking at the time), but we also wanted to make sure that it benefited small businesses. So we did it.

Besides providing information about local restaurants, bars and upcoming events, we also aim to help small local businesses create affordable marketing campaigns so that they can compete with the large corporations in town, such as Wal•Mart. (You'll never see a Wal•Mart ad on our website. We promise.)

If you have ideas about how we can improve our website, we'd love to hear them. Really, we take your suggestions quite seriously. We're also very open to co-sponsoring events, so if you feel this would be helpful to your organization, contact us and we'll get it setup.

Thanks for using MeNomNom!
